Predicting Comfort (Airline)

Associated with Macquarie University

This project analyzes the Airline Passenger Satisfaction dataset to enhance customer service, boost operational efficiency, and support strategic decision-making. Key objectives include:

Role

▪ Customer Service Improvement: Identified factors influencing satisfaction and targeted areas like on-board services, check-in, and in-flight amenities.
